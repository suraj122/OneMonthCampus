Location

Everything you need to know aboutOneMonthCampus.

OneMonthCampus is being organised in Ghoomakad.

How to rach?

By Flight

There are a few flights to Dharamsala (DHM) from Delhi (DEL) everyday. From Dharamsala airport, a taxi to Ghoomakad costs about 600 Rs. Please tell the taxi driver to take you to Ghoomakad at Rakkar-Sidbari.

By Bus

There are regular buses from Delhi and Chadigarh to Dharamsala. Once hyou reach Dharamsala, you can either take a bus to Sidbari and then walk up 2 kms or take a taxi directly to Ghoomakad (300 Rs.). You can book the tickets online through (Redbus, Paytm)

By Train

The nearest train station is Pathankot/ Pathankot Cant (PTK/PTKC). Once at Pathankot, you can take a bus to Dharamsala (3 hours/ 120 Rs). Follow above instructions to reach Ghoomakad from Dharamsala.
