FAQ

Everything you need to know aboutOneMonthCampus.

Q. Who is it for?

A. Anyone willing to explore, learn, create and get an active social life. Could be a student, college graduate/dropout seeking direction in life, entrepreneur seeking a break from city life, remote worker/digital nomad wanting to get things done.

Q. What is the duration of the program?

A. 30 days. The campus will start from 17th April @ 9am and end on 16th May post breakfast.

Q. I am a remote worker/entrepreneur/digital nomad and I work on my own stuff. Can I still come?

A. Sure, pull along your friend too!

Q. I can't stay for the entire month. Can I join it for a few days?

A. Yes, you can join it for a few days too. Although we think it would be more effective if you stay the entire month.

Q. How many participants are we expecting?

A. We are expecting 15-25 participants.

Q. How to reach?

A. See Location.

Q. What to carry?

A. Your utility stuff, one or two warm clothes, shoes, and lots of enthusiasm.
